Cecile-Anne Sison


Cecile is a Canadian living in Chicago, who (like most people) really enjoys art, music and film.

She has lived in Hong Kong, the Philippines and US, working on films and projects as a photographer, a metadata specialist and a second assistant camera on a sitcom. While working for the Mellon foundation on a grant, she quickly realized that academia was where she belonged.
